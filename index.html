Roy Wood$

Background

Denzel Spencer otherwise known as his singing name "Roy Woods" is from Brampton, Ontario. He is signed to Octobers Very Own (OVO) Sound, the record label co-founded by rapper Drake, producer Noah "40" Shebib and Oliver El-Khatib. He mostly makes R&B style music, some reasons maybe because he was inspired by Michael Jackson and also Dance Hall Culture.


Life before being an artist

In some interviews he stated that he thought he would get into professional football before ever becoming a rapper/singer, many said he was very talented in football during his highschool years.

Once his career started

He's only started in the music business since 2014 and continues on to this day, on July 11, 2015 Drake premiered the first song "Drama" from Roy Woods 'EP Exis on Apple Beats 1 on OVO's radio show, that's when Woods started getting the audiences attention. Later on July 25, 2015 a second song titled "Get You Good" was premiered.

Debut Album

Today on December 1, 2017 Roy Woods released his debut album named "Say Less" many fans have been waiting for this album to be released since he leaked the date a couple months back on social media.
